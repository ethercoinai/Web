Workflow

Three-Stage Settlement

From demand posting to token settlement — fully automated and trust-minimized

1

Post Demand

Requestor locks ETHER tokens via TaskMarket contract and submits AI task requests. Tasks enter the public Order Book, distributed to Workers by attestation zone

2

Execute Task

Worker executes AI tasks off-chain, EtherFLOP (EFLOP) precisely measured, submitting ZK Proof, Optimistic Anchors, or TEE attestation to the Verifier contract on-chain

3

Settle & Mint

Upon verification, ETHER rewards are released automatically. Treasury repurchases and burns tokens, and the protocol burns a portion of ETHER from each settlement — a deflationary compute-driven model

Three Roles, One Ecosystem

🤖

Requestor

Compute Buyer
  • Submit AI training/inference tasks
  • Lock ETHER tokens as reward

  • Earn validation rewards + challenge fees

Deflation Mechanism

ETHER supply decreases with every settlement — compute drives deflation
Every task settlement burns a portion of the ETHER paid by the Requestor. No new ETHER is minted after the 10-year mining phase except the 0.1% perpetual incentive, ensuring the total supply trends downward over time as network usage grows.

All burns are recorded on-chain — fully auditable, transparent, and irreversible.
