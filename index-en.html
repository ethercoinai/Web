Workflow

Three-Stage Settlement

From demand posting to token settlement — fully automated and trust-minimized

1

Post Demand

Requestor locks ECT tokens via TaskMarket contract and submits AI task requests. Tasks enter the public Order Book, distributed to Workers by attestation zone

2

Execute Task

Worker executes AI tasks off-chain, generates PoRW verifiable execution data, and submits ZK Proof or TEE attestation to the Verifier contract on-chain

3

Settle & Mint

Upon verification, ECT rewards are released automatically. Treasury repurchases and burns tokens based on protocol revenue — a demand-driven deflationary model

Dynamic Monetary Policy

ECT issuance = Total Supply × (Task Growth ÷ Target Growth) × Base Inflation Rate
When compute demand exceeds the target, issuance increases to incentivize more node onboarding. When demand lags, the model shifts deflationary.

Treasury regularly repurchases ECT from the open market and burns tokens proportional to total compute completed — creating auditable on-chain burn records.
