Our Services

Full-stack compute services for all three roles — from AI task posting to on-chain settlement

AI Computing

Powerful distributed compute for AI developers and enterprises — supporting大规模 machine learning training, model fine-tuning, and real-time inference at 60-80% lower cost than traditional cloud providers.

Training Services

Distributed training with PyTorch, TensorFlow, and other major frameworks, automatically scheduled across global GPU nodes. Supports single-node multi-GPU and multi-node distributed training for LLM fine-tuning and diffusion model training.

Inference Services

High-performance inference via vLLM and llama.cpp, supporting Llama 3, Mistral, Qwen, and other leading open-source models. Token-based billing with elastic scaling for production-grade AI deployments.

For Requestors

Complete task lifecycle management for AI compute buyers. Submit tasks through the TaskMarket contract, lock ECT tokens, choose proof types — fully traceable and verifiable. Supports ZK zero-knowledge proofs, TEE trusted execution, and Hybrid verification modes.

For Workers

One-stop node onboarding, task matching, and reward settlement for compute providers. Supports heterogeneous devices including PCs, servers, and edge devices. Plug and mine — earn ECT tokens proportional to your contribution.

  • One-click onboarding script with auto hardware detection
  • Intelligent task matching for optimal compute utilization
  • Real-time earnings dashboard tracking compute contribution
  • Reputation system — higher quality work earns higher rewards

Verification & Settlement

Attestors participate in PoRW probabilistic redundancy consensus and cross-validation to maintain network honesty. Ethercoin's Trusted Compute Escrow contract ensures payments are released only upon correct task completion.

Enterprise Integration

Custom integration services including private cluster onboarding, hybrid cloud deployment, and data security compliance. Supports AWS Nitro Enclaves, Intel SGX, and other TEE environments for finance and healthcare privacy requirements.
